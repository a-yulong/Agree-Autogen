The ADIRU component shall define the implementation-level data routing and execution constraints for ADIRU.impl. In the implementation layer, ADIRU.impl shall route signal flow from acc_pr.acc1_output to acc_hm_pr.acc1_input through C1 and signal flow from acc_pr.acc2_output to acc_hm_pr.acc2_input through C2. The deployment mapping shall bind acc_pr to processor main_cpu.part1, bind acc_hm_pr to processor main_cpu.part2, bind ADIRUp_pr to processor main_cpu.part3, and bind SHM_pr to processor main_cpu.part4.
