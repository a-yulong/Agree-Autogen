The GCAS_Monitor component shall define vertical acceleration as 100.0, expected impact time as 15.0, and recovery time as 5.0. In its implementation, the altitudeFromGround variable shall be computed by invoking the Agree_Nodes::height function using the current altitude, vertical velocity, and a minimum height time of 0.5 seconds. Furthermore, the component must guarantee that when the altitude input is less than or equal to 30.0 or the computed altitudeFromGround is less than or equal to 500.0, the gcasRequested output signal shall be true.
