The Aviate_Decider component shall maintain an internal variable numberServicesTrue that counts the total number of currently active service requests. The component shall implement dynamicDecide logic such that when the GCAS estimated time of impact (inpGCASImpact) is greater than the ACAS estimated time of impact (inpACASImpact) and the sum of their recovery times (inpGCASRecovery + inpACASRecovery) ensures no secondary collision occurs, the decision shall be ACAS; otherwise, the decision shall be GCAS. The final output outDec shall use the dynamic decision result when the value of numberServicesTrue is greater than or equal to 2; otherwise, outDec shall fall back to a static priority-based decision.
