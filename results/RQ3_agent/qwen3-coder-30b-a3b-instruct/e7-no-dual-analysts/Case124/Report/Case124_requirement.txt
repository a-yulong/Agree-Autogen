The MAGNETO_SUB_wrapper component shall define the implementation-level signal routing for MAGNETO_SUB_wrapper.i. In the implementation layer, MAGNETO_SUB_wrapper.i shall route data flow from Spg.mag_out_data_x to Mag_Out_Data_X through P1 and data flow from Spg.mag_out_data_Y to Mag_Out_Data_Y through P2.
