The Speed_Control component shall implement PID control logic using proportional gain P = 1.0, derivative gain D = 0.0, and integral gain I = 0.0. The component shall compute the error e as the difference between the target speed input and the actual speed input. It shall calculate the error integration term e_int as the integral of e over time and the error derivative term e_dot as the derivative of e with respect to time. The Speed_Control implementation shall ensure that the Actuator_Input output is strictly equal to the control law result P * e + D * e_dot + I * e_int.
