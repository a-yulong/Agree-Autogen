The environmental hypothesis for the TCAS_singleton component requires that the nearestIntruderAltitude parameter remains constant during the simulation, and that the following conditions hold: the nearestIntruderAltitude, ownAltitude_in, and nearestIntruderGroundDistance are all greater than 0; the ownHeading is between 0 and 360 degrees; the ownLatitude is between 0 and 90 degrees; and the ownLongitude is between -180 and +180 degrees.
