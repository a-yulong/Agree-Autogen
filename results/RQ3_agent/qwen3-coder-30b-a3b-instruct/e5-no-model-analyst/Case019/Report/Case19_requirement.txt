The GCAS_Recovery component shall define an altitude variable and invoke the gcasDynamics3 function for recursive computation. The component shall assume that the initial time timeRangeInitial and the final time timeRangeFinal are within the range [0.0, 10.0]. Under the assumption that the initial flight speed is 150.0 feet/second and the recovery time exceeds the threshold of 8.9 seconds, the component shall guarantee that the computed altitude altitude after recovery is greater than 0.0 to prevent ground impact.
