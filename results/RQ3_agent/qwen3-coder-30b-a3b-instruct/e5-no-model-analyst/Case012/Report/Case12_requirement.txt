The GCAS_Monitor component must establish a terrain clearance buffer logic (TCB) through guarantee statements. Specifically, when the verticalDistance input is less than or equal to 30.0 or the horizontalDistance input is less than or equal to 30.0, the component must transition into a collision avoidance state. It is assumed that the aircraft's roll rate remains within the range of 10.0 to 15.0. Additionally, the output ports outImpact and outRecovery must be initialized to 0.0 and must remain constant while the component is active.
