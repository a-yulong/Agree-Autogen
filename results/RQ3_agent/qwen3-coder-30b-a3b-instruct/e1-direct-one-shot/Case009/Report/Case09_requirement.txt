The environmental hypothesis specification for the TCAS_singleton component requires that the nearestIntruderAltitude parameter remains constant throughout the simulation. It further assumes that the following variables are greater than zero: nearestIntruderAltitude, ownAltitude_in, and nearestIntruderGroundDistance. Additionally, the geographical coordinate ranges for the own ship are constrained as follows: ownHeading must be between 0 and 360 degrees, ownLatitude must be between 0 and 90 degrees, and ownLongitude must be between -180 and +180 degrees.
