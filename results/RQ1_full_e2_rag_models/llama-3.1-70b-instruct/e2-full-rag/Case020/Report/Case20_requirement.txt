The temperature_sensor component shall ensure that the value of its output port dataout remains within the closed interval [0, 20]. Additionally, the component shall maintain an internal variable k, initialized to 10, which updates each cycle according to the following rule: if the previous cycle's k value is greater than or equal to 20, then k shall be decremented by 1; if the previous cycle's k value is less than or equal to 0, then k shall be incremented by 1; otherwise, k shall remain unchanged. An assertion shall be included to verify that the value of the output port dataout matches the current value of the internal variable k.
