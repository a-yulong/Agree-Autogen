The TrajectoryExtrapolation component shall define a logical variable intruderVerticalVelocity to represent the rate of change in altitude of the intruder, calculated as the difference between the current nearestIntruderAltitude and its value at the previous time step. The component shall predict the future intruder altitude using linear kinematics by assigning the sum of the current nearestIntruderAltitude and intruderVerticalVelocity to the projectedIntruderAltitude output port.
