The Car component shall assume that the input data Target_Speed.val is always non-negative and less than 150.0. The system shall define the const_tar_speed attribute to indicate the logical state of the target speed being constant. As a guarantee, when const_tar_speed is true, the output data Actual_Speed.val shall not exceed Target_Speed.val, and the absolute value of the difference between Actual_Speed.val in consecutive cycles (representing acceleration) must be strictly less than the constant max_accel, which is 40.0.
