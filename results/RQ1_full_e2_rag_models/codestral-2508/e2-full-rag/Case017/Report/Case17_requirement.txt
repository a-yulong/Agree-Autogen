The Aviate_Decider component shall maintain an internal variable numberServicesTrue that counts the total number of currently active service requests, where an active request is defined as a true value among the following inputs: inpGCASM, inpSAM, inpACASM, inpGFM, inpWxM, and inpFLS. The component shall implement dynamicDecide logic such that when inpGCASImpact is greater than inpACASImpact and the sum of inpGCASRecovery and inpACASRecovery is sufficient to prevent a secondary collision, the decision shall be ACAS; otherwise, the decision shall be GCAS. The final output outDec shall be set to the dynamic decision result when numberServicesTrue is greater than or equal to 2; otherwise, outDec shall default to a static priority-based value.
