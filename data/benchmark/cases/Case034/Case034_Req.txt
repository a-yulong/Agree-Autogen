The ResponseSelection component shall prioritize aircraft based on geographic longitude when resolving altitude conflicts. Specifically, when a threat is detected and the own aircraft's altitude is equal to the threat's altitude, the component shall select the maneuver command from the maneuver templates based on the longitudinal position: if the own longitude is greater than the threat longitude, the command for climbing (50) shall be selected; otherwise, the command for descending (-50) shall be selected. When the own aircraft's altitude is lower than the threat's altitude, the component shall output the top intrusion avoidance command (-10). When the own aircraft's altitude is higher than the threat's altitude, the component shall output the bottom intrusion avoidance command (10). The component shall receive inputs including threatDetected (Boolean), threatAltitude (Float), ownAltitude (Float), ownLongitude (Int), and threatLongitude (Int), and shall produce the requiredVerticalVelocity (Int) output.
