The GeoFence_singleton component shall establish a real-valued recursive variable t with an initial value of 0.0 and a step size of 0.1. At the implementation level, the component shall invoke the circleLinearization function from the geofence_nodes library, passing t as a parameter, to compute the distanceFromFence variable. The component shall set the value of the output port timeToRecovery to 1.7. The component shall guarantee that when the simulation time t reaches or exceeds the recovery time, the distanceFromFence at the current moment is greater than the distanceFromFence at the previous moment, ensuring the system executes a recovery action that moves the system away from the geofence boundary.
