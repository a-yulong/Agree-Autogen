The GCAS_singleton component shall maintain a real-valued recursive variable t with an initial value of 0.0 and a step size of 0.1. It shall define the constant minHeightAllowed as 200.0. At the implementation level, the component shall invoke the kinematicLinearization function from the gcas_nodes library, passing t as a parameter, to compute the height variable. The component shall set the output port timeToRecovery to the constant 3.7. The component shall guarantee that when the simulation time t reaches or exceeds 3.7, the computed height shall always be greater than minHeightAllowed, and the current height shall be greater than the previous height to indicate a pull-up trend.
