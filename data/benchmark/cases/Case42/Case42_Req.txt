The Microwave_Control component shall define the implementation-level data routing and execution constraints for Microwave_Control.Impl. In the implementation layer, Microwave_Control.Impl shall route signal flow from clear to MC.clear through clear_TO_MC_clear and signal flow from clear to DC.clear through clear_TO_DC_clear. Additionally, Microwave_Control.Impl shall use immediate communication timing on connection clear_TO_MC_clear, use immediate communication timing on connection clear_TO_DC_clear, use immediate communication timing on connection start_TO_MC_start, and use immediate communication timing on connection kp_0_TO_DC_kp_0.
