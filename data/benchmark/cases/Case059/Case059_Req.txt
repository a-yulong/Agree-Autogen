The BSCU component shall define the implementation-level data routing and execution constraints for BSCU.BSCU_Impl. In the implementation layer, BSCU.BSCU_Impl shall route signal flow from Panel to COM1.Panel through panel_to_COM1 and control signal from COM1.CMD to MON1.CMD_From_COM through COM1_to_MON1. Additionally, BSCU.BSCU_Impl shall use immediate communication timing on connection panel_to_COM1, use immediate communication timing on connection COM1_to_MON1, use immediate communication timing on connection pedal_to_COM1, and use immediate communication timing on connection fail_to_COM1.
