The Supervisor component's timeToFailure output shall be equal to the minimum of the three subsystems' timeToFailure inputs: gcas_timeToFailure, tcas_timeToFailure, and geof_timeToFailure. The implementation shall invoke the composite_nodes library to calculate three concurrency factors using the concurrencyFactor function, where the inputs are the respective subsystems' recovery times (gcas_timeToRecovery, tcas_timeToRecovery, geof_timeToRecovery) and the system's timeToFailure. The resulting concurrency factors shall be assigned to the output ports p1, p2, and p3 respectively. Additionally, the system shall guarantee that the timeToFailure output is greater than the sum of the three subsystems' timeToRecovery inputs.
