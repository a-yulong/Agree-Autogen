The temperature_sensor component shall ensure that the value of the dataout port remains within the closed interval [0, 20]. The implementation of temperature_sensor shall define an internal variable k, initially set to 10. For each execution cycle, the value of k shall be updated according to the following rule: if the previous cycle's k is greater than or equal to 20, then k shall be decremented by 1; if the previous cycle's k is less than or equal to 0, then k shall be incremented by 1; otherwise, k shall remain unchanged. Additionally, the value of the dataout port shall be equal to the current value of the internal variable k.
