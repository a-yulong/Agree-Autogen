The GYRO_SUB_wrapper component shall define the implementation-level signal routing for GYRO_SUB_wrapper.i. In the implementation layer, GYRO_SUB_wrapper.i shall route data flow from Spg.gyro_out_data_x to Gyro_Out_Data_X through P1 and data flow from Spg.gyro_out_data_Y to Gyro_Out_Data_Y through P2.
