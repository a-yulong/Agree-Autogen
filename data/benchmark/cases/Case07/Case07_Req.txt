The Supervisor component shall compute the system's timeToFailure as the minimum of the three subsystems' timeToFailure ports: gcas_timeToFailure, tcas_timeToFailure, and geof_timeToFailure. It shall invoke the composite_nodes library's concurrencyFactor function three times, using the respective subsystems' timeToRecovery values and the computed system timeToFailure to calculate concurrency factors, assigning the results to the output ports p1, p2, and p3. The Supervisor shall guarantee that the system's timeToFailure is greater than the sum of the three subsystems' timeToRecovery values.
