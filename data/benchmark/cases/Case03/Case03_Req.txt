The GeoFence_singleton component shall establish a recursive variable t with a time step of 0.1, declare the variable distanceFromFence, and compute distanceFromFence by invoking the circleLinearization function from the geofence_nodes library with the time variable t as its input parameter. The component must guarantee that, once the time t exceeds the recovery time value of 1.7, the computed distanceFromFence shall remain greater than its preceding time-step value. Additionally, the system failure time shall be set to 9.5.
