The GCAS_singleton component shall declare a height variable and invoke the kinematicLinearization function from the gcas_nodes library to compute its value. The component shall set the recovery time to 8.7 and the failure time to 16.0. Additionally, the component shall guarantee that once the simulation time exceeds the recovery time of 8.7, the height value must always remain greater than the minimum allowed height of 200.0, and the current height must be strictly greater than the height value from the previous time step to indicate a continuous pull-up trend.
