The ACAS_Monitor component shall set the system collision time constant to 8.0 and the recovery time constant to 5.0. When the value of the distance input port is less than or equal to 10.0 feet, the ACAS_Monitor component shall ensure that the acasRequested output port is true. During simulation, the value of the outImpact output port shall transition from 0.0 to the constant collision time constant value of 8.0, and the value of the outRecovery output port shall transition from 0.0 to the constant recovery time constant value of 5.0.
